KOLKATA

When you hit a sweet tooth you might wanna crave sweets and with the word “sweet” Kolkata comes to our mind. To buy sweets the best place is LovelySweets and LovelyBakeStudio. They have a vast variety of sweets and cakes at a very reasonable price. You can find LovelySweets inside uni-mall and LovelyBakeStudio near the staff apartment. The time you can go is 9:00 am to 10:00 pm.
