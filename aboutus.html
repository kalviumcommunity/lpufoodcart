About Us

Kalvium started FOODFOLIO because we aim to make food-hunting easy and enjoyable! Lovely Professional University boasts a food stall count of 100+ stalls but most of the students do not know where to locate or where they can get the most budget-friendly food from. This is where FOODFOLIO comes into play! To learn more about Kalvium’s functioning and student insights regarding the educational programme, visit Kalvium’s official blog website : Kalvium Matters
